TWDCC Adult Classes

TWDCC provides a variety of classes for adult students and teachers for ongoing dance & cultural education and community. 
Home to Diaspora Performance Project Artists.

TWDCC Youth Program

TWDCC provides dance education to dedicated and talented students, linking them to accomplished teaching artists. 

Performance

Supporting dance artists of all genres to tell their stories on as many platforms as possible. We elevate BIPOC voices and work to break through established forms of representation in all creative possibilities. 

Studios

TWDCC is a studio, performance, and cultural hub housed inside the Tannery Arts Center.

WinterDance Fest 2022 features premieres from a cast of world-renowned and emerging choreographers in a COVID-safe outdoor performance, downtown, on the roof of the parking structure at Walnut and Cedar streets.

WinterDance Fest 2022 will feature returning artists Gregory Dawson (dawsondancesf), Taliha Abdiel (Abdiel Dance Project), Angela Chambers (TWDCC), and WDF debuts by Evita Re (LINES Ballet), and Alexandra Mannings in collaboration with Kat Brault.

WinterDance Fest 2022 centers and highlights Black choreographers in the midst of Santa Cruz-wide Dance Week. WDF 2022 presents highly technical and expressive forms of contemporary dance from across a spectrum of traditions in a familiar but innovatively-used venue.

Thank you to our sponsors: Treehouse Dispensary (C10-0000105-LIC), Verve Coffee, Alderwood Santa Cruz, Stripe Design Services, Humble Sea Brewing, & Get Piped Plumbing.

Black Health Matters Initiative: One Year Anniversary!
October 16th
1PM-5PM

On October 16th from 1-5pm, Santa Cruz County’s Black Health Matters Initiative will celebrate its One-Year Anniversary with a day-long festival on the Tannery Arts Center campus featuring performances, live music, food, a health clinic, artists, family fun activities, and vendors. The BHMI One-Year Anniversary is open to all community members, and is free of charge.

PIVOT: The Art of Fashion
October 23rd
7:00PM

After the tremendously difficult time of Covid, heartache, fear and isolation, we all need art more than ever… art that challenges us, art that makes us laugh and art that takes our breath away. Pivot: The Art of Fashion is all that. Join us for a feel good time, in the courtyard at the Tannery Arts Center in Santa Cruz, CA on October 23. Pivot: The Art of Fashion's productions features designer’s fashions and artist’s wearable artwork in their inimitable style, with attention to detail and plenty of surprises. You don't want to miss this! Pivot… Art that brings us together. Ready… Set… Pivot!

Black Ballerinas: My Journey to Our Legacy with Misty Copeland
November 4th
5PM
Ticketed virtual Event

Bookshop is delighted to welcome bestselling and award-winning author and American Ballet Theatre principal dancer Misty Copeland for an online discussion of Black Ballerinas: My Journey to Our Legacy, an illustrated nonfiction collection celebrating dancers of color who have influenced her on and off the stage.
This event is cosponsored by Tannery World Dance & Cultural Center and NAACP Santa Cruz County Branch. TICKETS INCLUDE A COPY OF BLACK BALLERINAS WITH A BOOKPLATE SIGNED BY MISTY COPELAND!
